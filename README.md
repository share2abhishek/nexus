## What is NEXUS?

**NEXUS is a free, privacy-first AI digital marketing platform** that runs as a
desktop app on Windows. It generates marketing content, schedules and publishes to
social media, writes SEO copy and email campaigns — powered by a **local AI model
(Ollama) that runs on your own computer**, so there are no per-message fees and your
data never leaves your machine.

Looking for an **offline / local alternative to cloud AI marketing tools** that
keeps your data private and your costs at zero? That's NEXUS.

---

## ✨ Key features

- 🤖 **Local & free AI** — runs Llama 3.2 via [Ollama](https://ollama.com); no API
  keys or subscriptions required (cloud Google Gemini & Anthropic Claude optional).
- ✍️ **AI content generation** — posts, captions, ad copy, blogs, and emails.
- 📅 **Social media scheduler** — plan and auto-publish to LinkedIn, Facebook,
  Instagram, X/Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube, TikTok, and Google Business Profile.
- 🔍 **SEO tools** — keyword and on-page SEO content assistance.
- 📧 **Email marketing** — compose and send campaigns over your own SMTP.
- 🔒 **Privacy-first & offline-capable** — your data and AI stay on your device.
- 🖥️ **One-click Windows app** — bundled runtime, no separate Node.js install.
- 👥 **Multi-user** — each teammate registers with their own keys and accounts.

## ⬇ Download & install

1. Open the [**Releases page**](https://github.com/share2abhishek/nexus/releases/latest).
2. Download:
   - **`NEXUS-Setup-2.0.0.exe`** — installer with shortcuts *(recommended)*, or
   - **`NEXUS-Portable-2.0.0.exe`** — portable, just run it.
3. Run it. On first launch Windows SmartScreen may warn (the app isn't
   code-signed yet) → **More info → Run anyway**.

No Node.js or extra setup needed — everything is bundled.

---

## 🤖 First run — set up local AI (one time)

NEXUS opens a guided **"Set up your AI"** wizard:

1. **Install Ollama** → <https://ollama.com/download> (free; starts automatically).
2. **Confirm it's running** (green indicator).
3. **Download the model** — NEXUS recommends one for your hardware:
   `llama3.2:1b` (~1.3 GB, low-RAM PCs) or `llama3.2:3b` (~2 GB, 8 GB+ RAM).
   *(Manual: `ollama pull llama3.2:3b`)*

Then click **Start using NEXUS**. Your AI is now local, free, and private.

## ❓ FAQ

**Is NEXUS free?**
Yes. The app is free to download, and with local Ollama AI there are no usage fees.

**Do I need an OpenAI/Gemini/Claude API key?**
No — the local AI works without any key. Cloud keys (Google Gemini, Anthropic
Claude) are optional fallbacks you can add in Settings.

**Does my data stay private?**
Yes. With local AI, content is generated on your computer and isn't sent to any
cloud service. Your databases and settings stay in your Windows user folder.

**Can I run AI locally / offline on Windows?**
Yes. NEXUS uses Ollama to run open models like Llama 3.2 locally. After the
one-time model download, generation works offline.

**Which social platforms are supported?**
LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube, Google Business
Profile, and TikTok.

**How do I update?**
Download the newest installer from the
[Releases page](https://github.com/share2abhishek/nexus/releases/latest).

**I get "server process failed to start" / node.exe ENOENT.**
Fixed in v2.0.0 — install the latest release.
